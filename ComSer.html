Community Service Club

Robotics is changing industries from healthcare to manufacturing. The robots of tomorrow will be smarter, faster, and capable of more than ever beforeThe Community Service Club is dedicated to making a positive impact on society through volunteer work and outreach programs. Members engage in activities such as organizing charity events, environmental clean-ups, and educational initiatives for underprivileged communities. The club fosters a spirit of empathy, leadership, and civic responsibility by encouraging students to contribute to social causes and support those in need.
