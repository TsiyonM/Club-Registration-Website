Aerospace Club

The Aerospace Club focuses on aeronautics, space exploration, and aviation technologies. Members participate in projects related to aircraft and spacecraft design, drone building, and rocketry. The club hosts workshops, guest lectures, and competitions that help students understand the principles of flight, propulsion systems, and emerging aerospace technologies. It is an ideal place for those passionate about aviation and space science.
