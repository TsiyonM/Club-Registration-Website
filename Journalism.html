Journalism Club

The Journalism Club provides a platform for students interested in writing, reporting, and media production. Members contribute to school newspapers, magazines, and digital platforms, covering news, events, and creative stories. The club also teaches essential skills in interviewing, investigative reporting, and editing, helping students develop a strong journalistic voice while keeping the school community informed and engaged.
