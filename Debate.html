Debate Club

The Debate Club is a space for students to develop critical thinking, public speaking, and argumentation skills. Members participate in structured debates, learn persuasive techniques, and engage in discussions on various global and social issues. The club fosters confidence, logical reasoning, and the ability to articulate ideas effectively, preparing students for academic and professional success.
