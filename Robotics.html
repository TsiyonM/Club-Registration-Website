Robotics Club

The Robotics Club is a hands-on learning environment where students design, build, and program robots for various applications and competitions. Members work with robotics kits, sensors, and coding languages to develop autonomous and remote-controlled systems. The club encourages problem-solving, teamwork, and innovation, offering opportunities to participate in robotics challenges, STEM fairs, and research projects. It is an ideal space for students passionate about engineering, programming, and artificial intelligence.
