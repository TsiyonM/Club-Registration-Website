AI club

The AI Club is dedicated to exploring the world of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science. Members engage in hands-on projects, workshops, and discussions on AI applications in various fields. The club provides opportunities to work with real-world datasets, participate in AI competitions, and collaborate on research projects. Whether beginners or experienced programmers, students can enhance their technical skills and gain insights into the future of AI.
